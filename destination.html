Paris, France

Paris, the capital city of France, is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. Known for its art, culture, and gastronomy, Paris is home to iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral.

  • Best time to visit: April - June, September - November
  • Famous Food: Croissants, Macarons, French Wine
  • Top Attraction: Eiffel Tower
